Deep Fried oreos

Recipe Description

Delicious deep fried oreos to satisfy your sweeth tooth.

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts of vegatable oil for frying
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons of vegatable oil
  • 1 cup of pancake mix
  • 1 package of oroes

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees F.
  2. Whisk milk,egg, and 2 teaspoons of vegatable oil in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in the package mix until no lumps remain.
  3. Dip cookies into batter one at a time and carefully place into hot oil. Fry in batches of 4 or 5 at a time until cookies are golden brown. Drain on paper towel before severing.
