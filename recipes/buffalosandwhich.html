Buffalo Sanwhich

Recipe Description

This is a crockput Buffalo chicken that will please those who love buffalo wings. It is great for football games.

Ingredients

  • 4 Skinless, bonless chicken breast haves
  • 1(17.5 fluid ounce) bottle buffalo wing sauce
  • 1/2 ounce package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts into the slow cooker; pour 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch dressing mix
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 7 hours.
  3. Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks. Stir in butter.
  4. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaining buffalo sauce.
