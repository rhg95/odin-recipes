Mac N Cheese

Recipe Description

This is a crock pot mac and cheese recipe. It is creamy and easy to cook in a slow cooker.

Ingredients

  • One 16 ounce package of elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 16 ounce package of shredded cheddar cheese
  • One 5 ounce can of evaperated milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 can of condensed cheddar cheese soup

    • Steps

    1. Fill a large pot with salted water and bring to boil.Cook pasta uncovered,stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite. Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker.

    2. Add butter to pasta stir until melted. Season with salt and pepper. Sprink about 1/2 of the cheddar cheese over the pasta and stir.

    3. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.

    4. Whisk milk and condensed soup together in a bowl untill smooth; sitr into posture mixture.

    5. Cook on low for 3 hours.
